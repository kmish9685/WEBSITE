Why Use an Age Calculator?

Knowing your exact age in years, months and days is required for government job applications, competitive exams (UPSC, SSC, NEET, JEE), scholarship forms, and passport applications in India.

What is the age limit for UPSC exam? +
For UPSC Civil Services, General category candidates must be between 21 and 32 years. OBC candidates get 3 years relaxation (up to 35) and SC/ST get 5 years (up to 37). Age is calculated as of the 1st of August in the year of examination.
How is age calculated for government exams in India? +
Age for government exams is typically calculated as of a specific date mentioned in the notification (e.g., 1st January or 1st August). Use our "Calculate Age As On" field to enter the specific cutoff date.
