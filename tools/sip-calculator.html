What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) is a method to invest in mutual funds by putting in a fixed amount every month. It leverages rupee cost averaging and power of compounding to build wealth over time.

What is a good SIP return rate to expect in India? +
Equity mutual funds in India have historically returned 12–15% annually over long periods (10+ years). Debt funds typically return 6–8%. For a conservative estimate, use 10–12% for equity SIPs.
Can I stop a SIP anytime? +
Yes, you can pause or stop a SIP anytime without penalty. However, the already-invested amount remains in the mutual fund and can be withdrawn subject to exit load and tax.
