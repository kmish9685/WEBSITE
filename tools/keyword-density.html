What is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears in your content relative to the total word count. Formula: (Number of keyword occurrences / Total words) × 100

Ideal keyword density for a primary keyword is 1-2%. Going beyond 3-4% risks being flagged as keyword stuffing by Google, which can hurt your rankings.
