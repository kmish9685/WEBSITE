Why Are Meta Tags Important for SEO?

Meta tags are HTML snippets in the <head> section of your web page that tell search engines (Google, Bing) and social platforms (Facebook, Twitter) what your page is about. A well-optimized meta title and description directly impact your Click-Through Rate (CTR) in search results.

Does Google still use meta keywords? +
No. Google has officially stated that it does not use the meta keywords tag for ranking. However, Bing and some other search engines may still consider them. It doesn't hurt to include them, but focus your SEO effort on title, description, and content quality.
