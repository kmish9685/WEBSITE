What is Title Case? +
Title Case capitalizes the first letter of every word. Example: "the quick brown fox" → "The Quick Brown Fox". Used in headlines and titles.
What is the difference between camelCase and PascalCase? +
camelCase starts with lowercase and capitalizes subsequent words: "myVariableName". PascalCase capitalizes every word including the first: "MyVariableName". Both are used in programming.
