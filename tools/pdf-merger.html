How to Merge PDF Files Online

  1. Click "Select PDF Files" or drag & drop your PDFs into the box above
  2. Arrange the PDFs in the desired order
  3. Click "Merge PDFs" — this happens entirely in your browser
  4. Download the merged PDF file
ℹ️ This tool uses the open-source PDF-lib library. No files are uploaded to any server. Your PDFs remain 100% private on your device.
Is there a limit on the number of PDFs I can merge? +
There's no hard limit on the number of files, but very large PDFs (50MB+) may take longer to process in the browser. For best performance, keep total size under 50MB.
Will the merged PDF maintain quality? +
Yes! PDF-lib merges PDFs losslessly, preserving all text, images, and formatting at original quality.
