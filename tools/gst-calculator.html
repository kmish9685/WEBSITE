What is the difference between CGST, SGST and IGST? +
CGST (Central GST) goes to the central government and SGST (State GST) goes to the state government. Both are charged for intra-state transactions at half the GST rate each. IGST (Integrated GST) is charged on inter-state transactions and goes to the central government.
How to reverse calculate GST from a total inclusive amount? +
Select "Remove GST" in our calculator above. Formula: Original Price = Total Amount × 100 / (100 + GST%). Our tool does this automatically.
