How to Compress an Image Online

  1. Click "Upload" or drag & drop your image (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  2. Adjust the quality slider (80% is usually a good balance)
  3. Click "Download Compressed Image"

Your images never leave your device — all compression happens in the browser using the Canvas API.

Does compression reduce image quality visibly? +
At 70-80% quality, the visual difference is almost undetectable while the file size reduces by 50-70%. Go below 50% for aggressive compression where some quality loss may be visible.
Why can't I compress PNG to smaller than JPEG? +
PNG is a lossless format, so our browser-based compressor works best with JPEG and WebP. For PNGs, convert to JPEG/WebP format using the output format selector above for the best size reduction.
