Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolsIndia ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the website toolsindia.in (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit ToolsIndia, we may automatically collect:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)
  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2.2 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Email address when subscribing to our newsletter
  • Information submitted via our contact form

Important: All tools on ToolsIndia process data entirely in your browser. No files, images, or text you process through our tools are ever uploaded to or stored on our servers.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on users' prior visits to our Site or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to our website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the Internet.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses data.

4. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., dark mode preference)
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics (if enabled) to understand site traffic
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense cookies for relevant advertising

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the Site.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the Site
  • To send newsletter emails (only if you subscribed)
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To analyze and improve site performance
  • To comply with applicable laws

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Google AdSense: For serving relevant advertisements
  • Google Analytics: For website analytics
  • Service Providers: Only as necessary to operate the Site
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order

7. Your Rights (Indian IT Act 2000 & GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of incorrect data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details below.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

ToolsIndia does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
