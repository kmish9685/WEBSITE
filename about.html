About ToolsIndia

Free, fast and privacy-first online tools — built for India, used by the world.

Our Mission

ToolsIndia was built with one simple goal: give everyone access to powerful online tools for free. Whether you're a student calculating your age for a government form, a small business owner checking GST, a blogger checking word count, or a designer compressing images — we've got you covered.

We believe powerful tools shouldn't sit behind expensive subscriptions. Every tool on ToolsIndia is free, works instantly in your browser, and requires zero signup.

Why Client-Side Tools?

All our tools process data 100% in your browser. When you compress an image or convert a PDF, that file never leaves your device. This means:

  • 🔒 Complete privacy — your files stay on your device
  • ⚡ Instant results — no upload/download wait times
  • 🌐 Works offline after loading
  • 🆓 Free forever — no server costs = free for you

What We Offer

  • Image Tools: Compress, resize, crop and convert images
  • PDF Tools: Merge PDFs directly in your browser
  • Text Tools: Word count, case converter, duplicate remover
  • SEO Tools: Meta tag generator, keyword density checker
  • Finance Calculators: EMI, GST, SIP, Percentage, Age calculator (India-focused)
  • Multi-Language: Tools and articles available in Hindi, with more languages coming

Our Content

Our blog covers topics relevant to Indian readers — personal finance, government exam preparation (UPSC, SSC, NEET, JEE), health & wellness, and technology guides. All articles are original, well-researched, and regularly updated.
